    • Surprise Gift: Free Tuition for All N.Y.U. Medical Students

      • “In the field of medicine, schools have become worried that students saddled with steep debt are increasingly pursuing top-paying specialties rather than careers in family medicine, pediatrics and research.”

        • This article led me to think about how actions are mostly primarily driven by economic aspects, even in choosing a medical profession. It is inspiring to see that NYU has taken a big step in removing the huge barrier in people pursuing their passions by making their medical school's tuition free.
